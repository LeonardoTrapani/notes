Adjacency matrix $A: V \times V$ matrix where:
$$A(v,w) = \begin{cases} 1 & v,w \text{ adjacent} \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

For simple graphs, the adjacency matrix is symmetric with zeros on the diagonal.
